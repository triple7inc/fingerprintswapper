What Is FingerprintSwapper?

FingerprintSwapper is a tool that allows automated browser environments (like Selenium, Puppeteer, Playwright, etc.) to spoof highly realistic fingerprints from real-world devices. It works by modifying the fingerprinting surface of your environment to match genuine profiles.

Planned Support

We plan to support the most popular frameworks, including:

  • ✅ Selenium (Python, Java, C#)
  • ✅ Puppeteer (Node.js)
  • ✅ Playwright (Node.js, Python, .NET)
  • ✅ Cypress (JavaScript)
  • ✅ WebDriverIO (Node.js)
  • ✅ TestCafe (JavaScript)

Continued Dataset Expansion

Even after the official release, FingerprintSwapper will continue expanding its dataset by collecting data from links embedded in user websites and our own sources.

Always Free

FingerprintSwapper will always be free for up to 10 requests per second (this limit may change in the future). This is why we collect data and encourage others to help us build a robust, diverse fingerprint dataset. Together, we’re making an unbeatable fingerprint swapping tool — free, forever.
